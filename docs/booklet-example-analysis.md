## Generator Implications
- Keep a season/template-family layer. Ordinary Time, Advent, Lent, Palm Sunday, Christmas, and Epiphany are visibly different.
- Treat `The Readings`, `Collect for Today`, creed/affirmation, prayers, peace, and preparation-of-gifts style blocks as core managed sections.
- Preserve stage-direction lines like `*Preset:*`, microphone cues, and manual logistics as human-authored text around the managed sections.
- Palm Sunday is structurally special enough to need its own family, not a minor variant flag.
- Reading Programs should remain a separate artifact family from the main leader booklet.
